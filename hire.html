Bike hire information

We offer a range of bikes and flexible hire options for every type of rider:

  • ID is required to hire our bikes. A passport or driver's licence along with a credit card deposit

  • 16 and 17-year-olds can ride unsupervised but cannot supervise under 16's
  • All bikes are to be returned/collected by 5pm or a second day will be charged
